# research-projects

what is it?

Industry related data is data that collects, analyzes and publishes data on groups of different businesses.It shows the statistics of a businesses data along the lines of employment, the size of the business (how large it is), the types of occupations in the business, and etc. Industry related data is used to figure out the company’s most valuable customers and create new experiences, services, and products for a company. It is represented through various graphs and charts.

who started this

Industry Related Data is very close to Data Science, and these both started in the 21st century (2001), when William S. Cleveland decided that there needs to be a plan for expanding the data and statistics for businesses.

Why does this exist, what do people gain from this?

This exist because of how high demand our economy is. The demand for information on goods and services from consumers is increasing. So because of this it is important for companies to have industry related data to keep track of these records when consumers ask for them.
